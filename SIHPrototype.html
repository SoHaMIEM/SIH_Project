Welcome to AICTE Portal

The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) is a statutory body in India that oversees technical education and management education in the country. They provide accreditation to institutions and have a portal that offers various services related to approval, accreditation, and monitoring of technical and management institutions. The AICTE portal is a platform where institutions can apply for approvals, submit reports, access guidelines, and stay updated on regulatory information related to technical education in India.
